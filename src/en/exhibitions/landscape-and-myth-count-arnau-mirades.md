Exhibition of _Landscape and myth of Count Arnau_ during [Mirades Photography Festival](https://www.festivalmirades.com/).
The month-log event featured a total of 25 exhibtions spread across different emblematic spaces of Torroella de Montgrí, l'Estartit, and Ullà.

During the weekends, different activities related to photography took place, such as talks, screenings, and a fair for photography authors and publishers.
