A triptych from the series _Vanishing Landscape_ was one of the artworks for sale at the fundraising auction, all of the proceedings were donated to [Nepal Chhimeki](https://chhimeki.org/), a Nepalese nonprofit organization made up of over 1700 female volunteers dedicated to improve the living conditions of thousands of women and children in Kathmandu.

The exhibition of the artworks and subsequent auction took place at [Ocaña](https://www.ocana.cat/en/) in Barcelona's Plaça Reial.
